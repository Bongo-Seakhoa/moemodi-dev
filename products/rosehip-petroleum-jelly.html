About this jelly

Slower, warmer, deeply comforting.

Rosehip is one of those ingredients that just feels comforting. Our Rosehip Petroleum Jelly captures that mood with a warm botanical character and a rich, slow-melting texture. It takes its time, and so should you.

Reach for the 250ml as a daily go-to, or step up to the 500ml family-size jar if you live somewhere dry, share with the household, or just believe that good things deserve generous packaging.

How to use

  1. Take a small scoop with a clean fingertip.
  2. Warm between fingers, then press into dry skin.
  3. Best as an evening or overnight layer for hands, lips and elbows.
  4. Especially welcome on cold mornings and after travel.

Rosehip character

Warm, grounded botanical notes that read like a quiet evening, not a perfume counter.

Good to know

Care, storage and a few honest notes.

For best results, keep the lid closed and store at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. Avoid using on broken or irritated skin. Patch test on a small area first if you have particularly sensitive skin.

This product is intended for everyday cosmetic skin care. It is not a medicine and is not designed to treat or cure any medical condition.
