About MOEMODI

Rooted in nature.
Guided by heritage.

MOEMODI is a South African natural products manufacturer producing botanical-inspired everyday skincare. Our range covers agave herbal lotion, aloe vera and rosehip petroleum jellies. Each one supports daily moisturising and a refreshing feel for the whole family.

The brand draws from Sesotho/Bataung values: respect, harmony, craftsmanship and protection. We work with reputable suppliers, formulate in small considered batches, and finish every bottle to be at home on the shelf as well as on the dressing table.
